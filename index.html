About Us

At Araku Coffee House, nestled in the lush hills of Araku Valley, we pride ourselves on being a haven for true coffee enthusiasts and seekers of soulful moments. We’re dedicated to delivering an exceptional coffee experience rooted in the rich traditions of tribal farming. Our cozy and welcoming space invites guests to relax, unwind, and savor the unique flavors of organically grown Araku coffee in complete comfort.

Our Menu

  • Signature

    Harvested from coffee terroirs shaded by pepper entwined silver oak trees and nurtured by well aerated rocky soils. Terroir characterised by lots of rocks, and highest percentage of silver oak trees. A blend of washed and natural coffees.

  • Selection

    Comes from nutrient-rich coffee plots closest to the Araku habitations. Diversified shade, with silver oak trees entwined with pepper vines. A blend of washed, honey and natural processes.

  • Grand Reserve

    Complex and artisanal, grown in high-altitude scree soils with bio-diverse conditions. This coffee consists of washed and natural process.

  • Micro Climate

    Grown in a rare terroir, like the riverine island of Baankubedda, distinguished by gentle slopes, diversely shaded highland microplots. This coffee is a natural process.
